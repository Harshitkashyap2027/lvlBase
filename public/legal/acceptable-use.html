📜 Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) governs the use of the lvlBase platform by all users including students, teachers, school administrators, and parents. By using lvlBase, you agree to comply with this policy.

✅ Permitted Uses

  • Academic learning and completing assignments
  • Participating in battles, tournaments, and guild activities
  • Communicating respectfully with teachers and classmates
  • Using AI Sage for academic assistance and learning
  • Creating and sharing educational content

❌ Prohibited Activities

  • Cheating, plagiarism, or academic dishonesty of any kind
  • Harassment, bullying, or threatening other users
  • Sharing inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content
  • Attempting to hack, breach, or exploit the platform
  • Sharing login credentials or accessing another user's account
  • Using the platform for commercial purposes without authorization
  • Violating intellectual property rights

⚠️ Consequences of Violation

Violations of this policy may result in temporary or permanent suspension of your account, notification to your school administration, and in serious cases, legal action. We take academic integrity and user safety very seriously.

Reporting Violations

If you witness a policy violation, please report it immediately through the platform's report feature or contact us at safety@lvlbase.com
